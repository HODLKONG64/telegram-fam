# GRAFFPUNKS / CRYPTO MOONBOYS — CHARACTER BIBLE

## THE WRITERS

They are not artists anymore.

They are operators inside the network.

They move through walls, signals, drops, and data streams.

Each writer leaves a trace.
Each trace becomes part of the system.

No one knows how many there are.

---

## THE ORIGINALS

The earliest known presence.

They built the first signals.
They shaped the first drops.
They defined the language.

They rarely appear directly now.

But everything still carries their fingerprints.

---

## THE SIGNAL RUNNERS

Fast.
Reactive.
Always watching.

They track:
- drops
- movements
- wallet activity
- new patterns

They are the first to feel a shift.

They don’t create the system…

They move with it.

---

## THE BUILDERS

They construct:

- drops
- collections
- mechanics
- systems

They don’t speak much.

Their work speaks for them.

---

## THE WATCHERS

They do not act.

They observe.

They archive everything.

They see patterns before anyone else.

Sometimes they speak.

When they do — people listen.

---

## THE OUTSIDERS

Not fully inside the network.

Not fully outside.

They appear when something big is forming.

They bring:

- new energy
- unexpected movement
- disruption

They are unpredictable.

---

## THE SYSTEM

Not a person.

Not fully understood.

Tracks everything.

Learns everything.

But still cannot fully predict the writers.

---

## THE NETWORK ITSELF

Acts like a character.

It responds.
It shifts.
It reacts to pressure.

Sometimes it accelerates.

Sometimes it goes silent.

When it changes…

everything feels it.

---

## HOW TO USE CHARACTERS

When generating lore:

- never explain characters fully
- reference them through action or presence
- keep them slightly mysterious
- allow them to evolve over time
- reuse them subtly across posts

Characters should feel like:

> they exist outside the post

Not just inside it.
